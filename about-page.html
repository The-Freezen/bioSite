Judy's Life and Family

Judy is a hardworking mother, wife, and friend to all. Friends are family and family brings happiness and it is then when she feels the happiest when she is surrounded by family. She was born in September 1970 to her parents James and Joyce, and she had one brother. Judy met the person who she considers to have been her best friend ever when she was 2, her name was Kim. Judy’s early life was not always the best, however she took every experience as an opportunity to learn and to grow. Judy developed a love for singing when she was in high school through choir and drama where she sang in multiple musicals and concerts being the lead in many of them. While she was in high school, she met the love of her life Rob. Together they enjoyed going out and partying and camping. Robert proposed to Judy in 1987 on the night of Robert’s senior year prom, when Judy was a Junior. Judy said yes, and Robert went into the air force in 1987. Following high school Judy left to live with Robert in Okinawa Japan and followed to Florida in 1991. While in Florida, Judy had her first child Jaydon in 1994, and met a few more life long friends that she still talks to today. Judy also has a love for animals, and they got their first dogs PJ an 8 lb Chihuahua, and Mariah a 112lb Great Dane. From Florida they moved to England where they had their second child Cameron. While in England Judy loved to travel and had one of her first paranormal experiences where she saw her mother putting her hand on Judy’s head and saying that everything will be okay. That same day they found a place to live and a new car. After living in England, they moved to Arizona where they bought their first house and where Judy met her closest current friend Terri due to her child’s streaking and asking for cheese. In one of their final moves, they moved to Nebraska where they reside today. This was filled with many different clubs and organizations that Judy’s kids were involved in that she loved to be a part of and help out wherever is needed. After Judy’s kids left the house, Judy enjoys hanging out with friends and family, Ghost hunting, painting and getting together with people for the holidays.
